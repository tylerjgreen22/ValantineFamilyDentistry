About Us

Dr. Matthew Valantine has recently partnered with Gentle Dentistry, a leading provider of comprehensive dental services. With over a decade of experience in family and cosmetic dentistry, We are committed to delivering gentle, personalized care tailored to each patient's unique needs. Together, we are proudly serving the St. George community, offering a wide range of services including preventative care, cosmetic enhancements, and restorative dentistry. Our patient-centered approach ensures that every visit is comfortable and stress-free, making oral health a priority for the whole family.

Our Doctors

Dr. Matthew R. Valantine DDS

Dr. Valantine studied Biology and Psychology for his bachelors degree and attended dental school in San Francisco at the University of the Pacific Dental School, earning his Doctorate of Dental Surgery Degree. He practiced dentistry first in Riverside California before moving to St. George where he has practiced for 37+ years. More recently he has spent time doing humanitarian work in Haiti, Guatemala, China, Panama and Kenya. Dr. Valantine and his wife Jody have four children and fourteen grandchildren. He is excited to join with a team that values patient care in the same way he does.

Dr. Derek R. Evans DMD

Dr. Derek Evans, a skilled dentist with 14+ years in St. George, earned his Doctor of Dental Medicine in 2006 from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. Specializing in cosmetic and implant dentistry, he’s a member of esteemed dental associations, emphasizing high-quality care through continuous professional development.

Dr. Daniel B. Goold DDS

Dr. Daniel B. Goold’s professional journey includes a year of Advanced Education in General Dentistry in Las Vegas, where he gained expertise in IV sedation, surgical procedures, endodontics, and orthodontics. His dedication extends to military service, with two years in Germany and three in England, focusing on the dental well-being of military members and their families.

Dr. Brian Zwahlen DDS

Dr. Brian Zwahlen has been working as a dedicated dentist at Gentle Dentistry for the past three years, specializing in providing compassionate and comprehensive dental care. He holds a DDS degree from UCSF Dental School (2000) and a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics from BYU (1996).
